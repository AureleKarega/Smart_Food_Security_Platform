Our Mission

Where Climate Action Meets Food Justice

"We believe that food security and climate responsibility are inseparable. At ALU FoodShare, we empower students to tackle both crises simultaneously — by redistributing surplus food within our campus community, we reduce the environmental toll of food waste while ensuring no fellow student faces hunger. Every meal shared is a step toward a more just, sustainable Africa."
🌍

Climate Consciousness

Food waste generates 8-10% of global greenhouse gas emissions. By redirecting surplus food from landfills to plates, we directly reduce methane emissions and our campus carbon footprint.

🤝

Zero Hunger Campus

Aligned with SDG 2 (Zero Hunger), we create a safety net where every ALU student has access to nutritious food, regardless of their financial situation.

♻️

Circular Food Economy

We transform the linear "buy-consume-waste" model into a circular system where surplus becomes sustenance, building resilient food networks on campus.

How It Works

Share Food in 3 Simple Steps

1

Post Your Surplus

Have extra food from an event, cafeteria, or cooking? Snap a photo, add details, and post it on the food board.

2

Browse & Request

Students in need browse available food listings, filter by dietary needs, and request pickup.

3

Connect & Share

Meet at a designated campus pickup point, share the meal, and both earn impact points.
