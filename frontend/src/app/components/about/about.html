About Us

Fighting Food Waste.
Feeding Our Community.

ALU FoodShare is a student-led platform at the African Leadership University that addresses food insecurity while combating climate change through food waste reduction.

Our Mission

We believe that food security and climate responsibility are inseparable. At ALU FoodShare, we empower students to tackle both crises simultaneously — by redistributing surplus food within our campus community, we reduce the environmental toll of food waste while ensuring no fellow student faces hunger.

Every meal shared is a step toward a more just, sustainable Africa. Our platform is built on the understanding that climate change disproportionately affects food systems in Africa, and that local, community-driven solutions are essential to building resilience.

SDG Alignment

SDG 17
